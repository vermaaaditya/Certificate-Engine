# Design System Theme

## Colors
Our color palette is carefully selected to evoke a modern and dynamic feel.
- **Primary Color:** `#20d5c3` is used for primary actions and key brand elements, reflecting innovation and clarity.
- **Secondary Color:** `#2556e2` provides a strong contrast and is used for secondary actions and complementary elements, symbolizing stability and depth.
- **Tertiary Color:** `#000000` serves as an accent color for highlights or decorative elements, offering a sophisticated touch.
- **Neutral Color:** `#3f55ff` forms the base for backgrounds and surfaces, ensuring a clean and accessible user interface.
The color mode is set to `light`, providing a bright and airy aesthetic throughout the interface.

## Typography
We utilize a unified and modern typeface for readability and brand consistency.
- **Headline Font:** `publicSans` is used for all headlines, ensuring strong visual hierarchy and impact.
- **Body Font:** `publicSans` is chosen for all body text, providing excellent legibility for long-form content.
- **Label Font:** `publicSans` is also applied to labels, maintaining consistency across interactive elements.

## Shape
Our UI elements feature a maximum roundedness, giving them a soft, modern, and friendly appearance. This pill-shaped characteristic contributes to an approachable aesthetic.

## Spacing
We maintain a normal level of spacing between and within UI elements. This provides a balanced visual rhythm, preventing clutter while maintaining a clear and easy-to-read layout.
